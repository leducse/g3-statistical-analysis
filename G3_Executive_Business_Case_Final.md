## Methodological Strengths and Limitations

### What We Did Right
1. **Rigorous Matching:** 100% propensity score matching with excellent balance
2. **Data Quality Controls:** Removed extreme outliers while preserving business heterogeneity
3. **Multiple Methodologies:** Propensity scoring + difference-in-differences for validation
4. **Conservative Estimates:** Used realistic effect sizes rather than inflated metrics

### Acknowledged Limitations
1. **Sample Size:** 234 customers insufficient for statistical significance on small effects
2. **Observation Period:** May be too short to capture full revenue impact
3. **Selection Bias:** Despite matching, unobserved differences may remain
4. **External Factors:** Market conditions could influence results

### Statistical Power Analysis
- **Current Power:** ~30% to detect 6% effects
- **Required Sample:** ~800 customers per group for 80% power
- **Timeline:** 12-18 months to achieve adequate sample size
